    Social Media #EyeForHK
    A protester was shot in the head and lost her right eye permanently.
    1. Take a selfie with your right eye covered
    2. Upload the photo on social media
    3. Tag at least 4 friends to take the challenge
    4. Add the following hashtags:
      #EyeforHK #Eye4HK #NoPoliceBrutality #FreeFromFear #DemocracyForHK
    #ProtestToo
    Many Hong Kong protesters have been strip-searched, taunted, and abused by the police after being arrested for participating in pro-democracy protests.
    1. Post news articles, pictures, or videos related to the mistreatment of Hong Kong protesters on your social media account
    2. Use the following hashtags:
      #ProtestToo #MeToo #HongKongProtest
    3. Tell friends and family about what’s going on in Hong Kong and the injustice that still happen there
    4. Sign petitions
    #StandWithHongKong
    1. Post new articles, pictures or videos related to the Hong Kong protests and voice your support for Hong Kong
    2. Use the hashtag: #StandWithHongKong and/or #StandwithHK
    3. Tag at least 4 friends to take the challenge
    #PokemonForHK
    HongKongers have been drawing Pokemon fanarts on Twitter in support of the Hong Kong protests.
    1. Create art that relates to the Hong Kong protests
    2. Post with the hashtag: #PokemonForHK
